    Welcome to Glamor Cuts

    Glamor Cuts is a professional hair salon that has been serving the East Bay Area since 2007. The salon is owned and operated by Julia who holds over ten years of experience as a hair stylist. She is devoted to providing exceptional quality for every one of her clients. Julia is passionate about what she does and is honored to bring her skills and expertise to her clients to give them a great experience and the hair they desire.
